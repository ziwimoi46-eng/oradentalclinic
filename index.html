Technology & Safety

Advanced Equipment

We invest in the latest dental technology to deliver precise, safe, and comfortable treatments for every patient.

Dental Operating Microscope

A high-precision microscope used by dentists to clearly see very small details inside the tooth during treatment.

It provides strong magnification and bright light so the dentist can see tiny cracks, canals, and infected areas that are not visible to the naked eye.

Non-invasive viewing technology, improves treatment accuracy, and reduces unnecessary drilling.

Helps in more precise treatments, especially for root canal procedures, improving success rates and patient comfort.

Endomotor

A modern electronic device used during root canal treatment to clean and shape the root canals.

The motor rotates special dental files inside the root canal to remove infected tissue and prepare the canal for filling.

Controlled speed and torque settings prevent damage to the tooth and make the procedure safer.

Makes root canal treatments faster, more accurate, and more comfortable for the patient.

Rubber Dam

A protective sheet used to isolate the tooth being treated from the rest of the mouth.

It is placed around the tooth so that saliva and bacteria cannot enter the treatment area.

Prevents contamination, protects the patient from swallowing dental instruments or chemicals.

Keeps the treatment area clean and dry, improving the quality and safety of dental procedures.

Autoclaved / Sterilized Instruments

All dental instruments are sterilized using a medical autoclave machine before use.

The autoclave uses high-temperature steam under pressure to kill bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms.

Ensures 100% sterilization of instruments to prevent infection.

Protects patients from cross-infection and maintains the highest level of hygiene in the clinic.
